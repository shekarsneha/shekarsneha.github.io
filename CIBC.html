Project Overview

The Problem

How do we create tools to enable banking center employees to address clients while they're waiting in the lobby or in line for a teller? Can we service them earlier and address their needs using an iPad?


The Process

  • An Agile team using the common ceremonies associated with the Scrum methodology.
  • Daily 15 minute stand-ups are used to share team updates and help one another resolve any blockers.

I was involved with discovery sessions, grooming sessions and planning sessions throughout the project life cycle to help guide the direction of the sprints and what features would be added next. The product owner relied heavily on me to propose concepts for new features to be added into the project. Because of this expectation, I was also involved with ideating and finding the gap within the existing feature set.


The Team

Scrummaster, Product Owner, Development (4), Quality Assurance (2), User Experience (me!)


The Solution

A collection of iPad applications that can identify a client, provide an overview of their holdings and perform basic cashless banking transactions .

User Research and Testing

Baseline Observations

We job shadowed frontline staff at the banking center to understand how client interactions take place today. The information we wanted to collect included quantitative data on client demographics, transactions, and time spent in lobby, in line, and with a teller.
